Documents Checklist
  • Passport
    • Valid for at least 18 months beyond the intended arrival date
    • At least 2 blank pages
    • Original + photocopy of the main page
  • Official Invitation Letter
    • Issued by the Russian Ministry of Internal Affairs or your university
    • Required for applying at the consulate or embassy
  • Visa Application Form
    • Filled online at visa.kdmid.ru
    • Print and sign the form
  • Recent Passport-Sized Photos
    • 2 color photos (3.5 x 4.5 cm)
    • White background, matte finish
  • Academic Documents
    • Original + translated & notarized copies of:
    • School leaving certificate / Higher Secondary
    • University transcripts (if applying for PG)
  • Medical Certificate (Including HIV Test)
    • HIV-negative certificate (valid for 3 months)
    • General medical clearance
    • Must be translated into Russian and notarized
  • Accommodation or Address Proof (if required)
    • Confirmation from the university hostel or private accommodation details
  • Visa Fee Receipt
    • Payment receipt as per the consulate requirements
  • Travel Itinerary or Ticket Booking (Optional)
    • Some embassies may ask for a planned travel date
